Campaign Strategy

The Presidential
Roadmap

Three campaign sections. Concrete strategies. Measurable outcomes. This is governance, not campaigning.

Section 01

Academic &
Clinical Welfare

Strategies for better attachment coordination and resource access — ensuring every nursing student at Maseno receives the clinical exposure and academic support they deserve.

1.1
Digital Placement Registry via ClinPlacer

Implement ClinPlacer school-wide within 90 days, replacing manual systems with real-time placement tracking, supervisor contact management, and NCK-compliant hour logging.

1.2
Student Resource Access Fund

Establish a student-managed equipment lending programme for clinical tools (stethoscopes, BP kits, PPE) to close the resource gap for financially constrained students.

1.3
Welfare Review Standing Committee

A standing committee of students and faculty reviewing welfare cases monthly with transparent public reporting on all resolutions and pending actions.

Clinical Excellence
Every student. Every rotation. Fully supported.
Open Door Governance
No closed rooms. No hidden decisions.
Section 02

Transparency &
Open Governance

Monthly "Open Door" digital reports for student governance — because a student body that cannot see its leadership is a student body that cannot trust it.

2.1
Monthly Open Door Reports

A published digital report every month summarising all governance decisions, welfare actions taken, budget allocations — distributed to every enrolled student via the digital communication platform.

2.2
Live Budget Dashboard

All student council funds visible via a public digital dashboard, updated after every expenditure. No hidden transactions. No unapproved spend.

2.3
Quarterly Town Halls

Structured Q&A sessions open to all students, with questions collected digitally in advance to ensure every voice is heard — not just the loudest in the room.

Section 03

Digital Integration
& Communication

Leveraging technology to streamline student communications — because in 2025, a nursing school without digital communication infrastructure is leaving its students behind.

3.1
Student Communication App

A centralised student app for announcements, exam schedules, placement updates, and direct channels to student leadership — eliminating information silos entirely.

3.2
Digital Notice Board System

Replace physical notice boards with a mobile-accessible digital system ensuring time-sensitive information reaches students instantly, regardless of their location.

3.3
E-Learning Resource Hub

A curated shared digital library of clinical resources, past papers, WHO guidelines, Kenya KHSSP materials, and study tools — free and accessible to all enrolled students.

Digital-First Leadership
Technology in service of every student's learning journey.
