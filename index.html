The Mission

Healthcare Begins
with How We Train
Our Nurses

"The systems we build for students today become the infrastructure patients rely on tomorrow."

Elvis Kidagisa entered nursing training at Maseno School of Nursing and quickly identified the structural failures that had long been dismissed as normal — disorganised clinical placements, absent digital infrastructure, zero transparency in student governance.

Rather than accept these as permanent, he chose to build. ClinPlacer was the first proof-of-concept. His presidential campaign is the next phase.
